Fettuccine


the description of Fettuccine : is a type of long, flat pasta that originated in Italy. It is commonly served with creamy sauces, such as Alfredo sauce, or with meat and vegetab


Ingredients :

  • Fettuccine pasta (250 grams)
  • Heavy cream (1 cup)
  • Parmesan cheese (1/2 cup, grated)
  • Butter (2 tablespoons)
  • Garlic (2 cloves, minced)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish)

steps :

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water and cook the fettuccine pasta until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan over medium heat, melt the butter.
  3. Add the minced garlic and saut√© for about 1 minute until fragrant (be careful not to burn it).
  4. Pour in the heavy cream and bring it to a gentle simmer.
  5. Slowly add the grated Parmesan cheese, stirring continuously until the sauce becomes smooth and creamy.
  6. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  7. Add the cooked fettuccine to the sauce and toss to coat the pasta evenly.
  8. Serve immediately, garnished with fresh parsley.
